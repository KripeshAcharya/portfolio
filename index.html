About

Passionate about bridging the gap between emerging threats and cybersecurity solutions, I am dedicated to advancing the protection of digital infrastructures. Coupled with a Bachelor of Science in Computing (First Class Honors), this academic foundation is complemented by extensive practical experience, showcasing my commitment to excellence and innovation in the field. My certifications: ISC2 Certified in Cybersecurity, NIST RMF, Security Blue Team Junior Security Analyst Pathway, Cisco Junior Cybersecurity Analyst Career Path and ICSI CNSS demonstrate my commitment to staying ahead of the evolving cybersecurity landscape.

I have led key projects such as MITRE’s “Efficacy of LLM Powered Malware Obfuscation,” explored compounding threats with Sandia National Laboratories, and developed automation solutions for vulnerability scanning with the National Security Agency. My technical expertise includes Snort configuration, red team reporting, Raspberry Pi integrations, and cybersecurity awareness training. I have also engaged in vulnerability management, threat analysis, and leveraging open-source intelligence tools and network analysis to strengthen security postures.

Additionally, with two years as a Technical Support Executive in the ISP industry, I’ve developed advanced troubleshooting skills and a hands-on approach to network and systems security, further enriching my ability to address complex security challenges.
